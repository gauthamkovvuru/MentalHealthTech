Mental Health in the Tech Workplace (2014)¶

by Gautham Kovvuru¶

Introduction¶

This is a tutorial for data science in which we will work with data curation, parsing, and management; exploratory data analysis; and machine learning.

We will do this by working with data from a survey about mental health in the tech workplace from 2014.

Mental health is defined as our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. According to the Rhode Island Psychological Association, mental health is important because "when we are free of depression, anxiety, excessive stress and worry, addictions, and other psychological problems, we are more able to live our lives to the fullest". Nearly 1 in 5 Americans suffers from mental illness each year. This means that one fifth of the American population is not living their lives to the fullest.

If you're unfamiliar with mental health, take a look at this website to learn more: https://www.mentalhealth.gov/basics/what-is-mental-health/index.html

Looking at the correlation matrix, we can see that there is a very strong, positive correlation between the treatment and work_interfere variables, so people who get treatment tend to have mental health issues that interfere with work and vice versa.

There is also a strong, positive correlation between the variables, wellness_program and seek_help. This means that people with companies that have employee wellness programs tend to have employers that offer resources on how to seek help and vice versa.

There is also a strong, positive correlation between the mental_health_consequence and phys_health_consequence variables. This means that people that think there would be a consequence for discussing mental health with an employer also think the same for a physical problem and vice versa.

Also, there is a strong positive, correlation between the coworkers and supervisor variables. This means that people who would be willing to discuss mental health with their coworkers feel the same about their supervisor and vice versa.

There is also a strong, negative correlation between the variables, mental_health_consequence and supervisor. This means that if someone thinks that there will be consequences for talking about mental health with their employer then they will not be willing to talk to their supervisor about it and vice versa.

We're going to use the data to predict whether or not someone will get treatment for mental health or not. In order to do that, we're going to need to separate the treatment data from the rest of it. After that, we'll split the data into a training set and a testing set. We use the teseting set to mfind the patterns and we use the testing set to check how accurate our predictions are. In this situation, 75% of our data will go to training and 25% of it will go to testing.

We'll make a dataframe of the machine learning model and it's accuracy score (a percentage of how accurate the model is).

X = numbersdf.drop(['treatment'], axis=1)
y = numbersdf['treatment']
X_train, X_test, Y_train, Y_test = train_test_split(X, y, test_size=0.25, random_state=10)

ml_df = pd.DataFrame(columns=["Model", "Accuracy"])

def score(model):
    clf = model
    clf.fit(X_train, Y_train)
    return accuracy_score(Y_test, clf.predict(X_test))

i = 0
for name, model in models.items():
    accuracy = score(model)
    ml_df.set_value(i, "Model", name)
    ml_df.set_value(i, "Accuracy", accuracy*100)
    i += 1

Conclusion¶

Mental health is important and although people should be able to get treatment regardless of what company they work for, this is not the case. Clearly there are a lot of different factors that come into play that determine whether or not people get treatment for mental health. It is important that people who need treatment get it so that they won't have to deal with mental illness. Hopefully, this data analysis, visualization, and machine learning will be useful for people that may need mental health treatment. They can look at this when when making a decision to join a tech company and can use our machine learning model to see if they will be able to get treatment or not.
